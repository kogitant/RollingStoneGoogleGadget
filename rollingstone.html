RS Album Reviews Module/Gadget

Problem: When the rss feed containing the latest album reviews from (http://www.rollingstone.com/rssxml/album_reviews.xml) RollingStone.com is added to the Google Personalized Homepage (http://www.google.com/ig) it shows only the name of the artist and the album title.

Solution: Parse the feed with my own gadget to optionally allow the user to see album art, review text and how many stars the album got

Usage: To use the module, just click the button below to add it to your personalized homepage. That's it!
